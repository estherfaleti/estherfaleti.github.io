WINRY ROCKBELL

ウィンリィ・ロックベル - Winrī Rokkuberu

Winry is a main supporting character of the Fullmetal Alchemist series. She is Edward Elric and Alphonse Elric’s close childhood friend of their young life in Resembool and is almost like a sister to the two and essentially their only family. Both her aprents were very smart doctors and were drafated to work for the militraiy during the Ishval civil war, however this restulted in both their deaths. Due to this Winrey was raised by her grandmother Pinako, Winry is an amateur surgeon and mechanical repair specialist enthusiast, Winry is Edward's own personal automail engineer and mechanic, making sure his prosthetic right arm and left leg are always in good working shape. Additionally, she provides a great deal of emotional support for the pair and plays the role of Edward's love interest.
