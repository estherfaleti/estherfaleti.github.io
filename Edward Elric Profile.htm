EDWARD ELRIC

エドワード・エルリック - Edowādo Erurikku

Growing up with his mother Trisha Elric, father Van Hohenhime and younger brother Alphonse Elric, in the small country town of Resembool, East State of the country, Amestris. Life was all fine and they were all the happiest family.

But one day his father dissapreas without even a proper goodbye and they are now left with only their mother to take care of them with the hopes of him coming back.

However while waiting for his father in that time his mother passes away. Filled with resentment towards his father, Him and Alphonse promise to bring their mother back to life with the use of alchemy.

Be that as it may it is actally a taboo to bring someone back to life with the use of alchemy. The young boys have commited a crime which results in him losing his right arm and left leg.

So at the age of 12, Edaward and along with his brother Alphonse set out to find the legendary Philosopher's Stone and becomes the youngest state alchemist in order to restore his and his brothers body back.
